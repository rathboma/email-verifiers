How Email Verification Works

Email verification services check email addresses through a multi-step process without sending actual emails:

  1. Syntax check - Verifies the email follows the correct format
  2. Domain check - Confirms the domain exists and has valid DNS records
  3. Mailbox check - Connects to the mail server to verify the mailbox exists
  4. Additional checks - Identifies catch-all domains, disposable emails, role-based addresses, etc.

This process helps maintain a clean email list without risking your sender reputation through hard bounces.
